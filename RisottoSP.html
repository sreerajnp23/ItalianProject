Risotto

What is Risotto?

Risotto is a creamy Italian rice dish that is cooked slowly with broth and often includes ingredients like cheese, vegetables, or meat.

What ingredients do you need?

  • Short-grain Arborio
  • Rice
  • Broth
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • White Whine
  • Parmesan Cheese

What are the steps to making Risotto?

  1. Saute onions in butter unitl they're soft.
  2. Add rice and stir until its coated in the butter.
  3. Add white whine to pan and stir until absored.
  4. Gradually add broth to the pan, stirring constantly until each addition is absorbed.
  5. Finally stir in Parmesan cheese and any other ingredients you want to add.

Complete! Enjoy!
